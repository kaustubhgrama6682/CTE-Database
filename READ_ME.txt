Welcome to Cougar CTE: the Bothell High School CTE (Career and Technical Education) Department Data Web Application! This application provides a user-friendly interface for accessing and visualizing data related to the CTE department and its partnering organizations.

Introduction
Bothell High School's Career and Technical Education (CTE) Department is committed to preparing students for a wide range of high-demand careers by offering courses that blend academic rigor with real-world relevance. This web application serves as a hub for accessing and analyzing data related to the CTE department and its partnering organizations.


Features:




Data Display: Access information about courses offered by the CTE department, student enrollment statistics, and more.
Visualization: Visualize data through interactive charts and graphs to better understand trends and patterns.
Partner Organizations: Explore information about organizations partnering with Bothell High School's CTE department, including their contributions and involvement.
Search Functionality: Easily search for specific data points or categories within the application.
User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for easy navigation and accessibility.
